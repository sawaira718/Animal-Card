Tiger

Tigers are easily recognizable with their dark vertical stripes and reddish/orange fur.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Height : 70-120 cm
  • Average Lifespan : 10-15
  • Habitat: forests

Tigers are the largest cat species in the world reaching up to 3.3 meters in length and weighing up to 670 pounds! Subspecies of the tiger include the Sumatran Tiger, Siberian Tiger, Bengal Tiger, South China Tiger, Malayan Tiger and Indochinese Tiger.
